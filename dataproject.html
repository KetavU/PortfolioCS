Which type of gum lasts longest?

The question

If your the person who wants gum with long lasting flavor, then this is the perfect page for you! I did an experiment to help me make a recomendation on which gum to chose for longer lasting flavor. I experimented with three types of gum, Trident orange flavor, Orbit peppermint and Orbit spearmint. I tested each gum three times with one person over the span of three days to see how long the flavor lasts. Then I got the average of all three gums and made a graph. I hope my recomendation will help you choose which type of gum you want to buy.

What data I collected

How long each gum lasts? Which type of gum lasts longest? Which type of gum lasts shortest

Cleaning my data

Some of the problems I had while collecting data were.

Human error, the person collecting data might not have said exactly when the flavor ran out and I might not have hit the stop button on the timer at the correct time. Each peice of gum is different in size and weight. Some gums might of had more flavor packed into them.

My recomendation

As you see I graphed the average of each gum. Orbit Spearmint won the longest lasting flavor by a landslide. Each gum had numbers around the same as their averages for their three test. If you like fruity gum it is likely to have flavors that ast shorter because it have more sugar and aspartame. With minty flavors, you will see that it lasts longer. I think the Orbit Spearmint won because it has no sugar and very little aspartame. I hope this helped you decide which gum you want to get.

Automation

I would not automate my recomendation because these numbers probubly wont change much unsless the company adds some new ingredients in to their formula.If I tried this experiment some other time with a different person I should see similar results. So I dont think it should be automated.
